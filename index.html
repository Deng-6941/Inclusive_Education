Resources

We will include accessible learning materials
such as Braille books, audio books, large
print materials, and digital resources designed
for people with different types of disabilities.

We will invest in assistive technologies like screen
readers, speech-to-text software, and adaptive
devices to make learning more accessible for individuals
with disabilities.
